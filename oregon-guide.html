Must-stop highlights
Haystack Rock, Cannon Beach
Must-stop
The icon. Best at golden hour when the crowds thin and the light turns the rock orange. Low tide reveals tide pools full of sea stars and anemones.
🕐 Best: 1hr before sunset 🅿️ Free street parking
Thor's Well, Cape Perpetua
Must-stop
A saltwater drain in the ocean rock that "swallows" the sea. Looks like nothing at low tide. At mid-to-high tide with swell it's unlike anything else on the coast.
🌊 Tide timing critical 📍 See full guide for chart
Natural Bridges, Brookings
Hidden gem
Sea arches carved by waves with the Pacific visible through the openings. Most people drive straight past the pullout. Worth the short scramble down.
🥾 10 min scramble 📍 Mile marker 349
Astoria Column
Local tip
Most visitors skip it for the bridge view. Wrong move. The spiral staircase to the top reveals the Columbia River, the Pacific, and three states on a clear day.
🕐 20 min 💲 $5 parking pass
